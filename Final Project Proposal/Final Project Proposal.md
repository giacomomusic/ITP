### Paragraph Overview 

For this project I knew that I was at a point in my enineering career in which I wanted to build a website as I knew it was a great way to connect with other people I have met in the industry. I want to, as part of this project, gain an understanding of website building enough so that when I have projects I complete or new things to add, I will have places to put it, and an understanding of how to implement on my website. The other piece of this is I wanted it to expand to something even larger. My idea was to keep a log of everyone I have ask to do work. Maybe something like when they fill out the form that sends me an email it also imbeds the same information in a google sheet that updates with information and room for me to add more later (such as if the client and I actually worked together, on what, and to keep track if we do multiple projects). I think that this will be a great first step in my journey of having a very professional website but the extra touch of the database will make it stand out to myself (somethhing I couldn't just build with something like wix). 



#### Will anything overlap with other classes?

None of the work I am doing in this project will overlap with other classes, the only shared info might be the actual content within the Portfolio but it will not accomplish any outside work. 

#### GOOD outcome for final project? (Will Acomplish)

A good outcome for the final project will be to have a functioning website with everything ready to be able to have someone contact me and send me an email with important info for what they might need for a project. 

#### BETTER outcome for final project? (Think you can Accomplish)

A better outcome would be the website connected fully with a google sheet, or excel doc, that keeps track of everyone who contacts me and allows me to very quickly plan, keep track, and manipulate information very easily to keep a solid database of all my clients. 

#### BEST outcome for final project? (Hope to Accomplish)

The best would be the combination of the previous 2 outcomes plus making the website look really amazing and possibly interactive within the photo. I could imagine the whole landing page of the website being a studio with the monitor showing a video of something I created, the speakers when clicked on playing music from a recent project, the console bring up my mixing work, and other interactive parts rather than a typical landing page with home and pages.
